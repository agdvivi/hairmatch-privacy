HairMatch AI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when using HairMatch AI, including photos uploaded for hairstyle generation, authentication data (such as Google Login), and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your photos are used solely to generate AI hairstyle results. Photos are not stored permanently and are deleted after processing. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

We use Firebase to securely store your account preferences and usage history. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth for authentication and Google Gemini AI for image generation. Your use of these services is subject to Google's privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

HairMatch AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
